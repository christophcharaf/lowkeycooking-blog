Sometimes, after a long day, I crave something that feels both indulgent and incredibly easy to make. This Lemon Herb Roasted Salmon with Asparagus is my go-to for just that! I remember the first time I made it, I was surprised by how quickly it came together, yet tasted like something from a fancy restaurant. Don't let the elegant presentation intimidate you; home cooks and starter cooks will find this recipe remarkably simple, delivering maximum flavor with minimal effort.

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs salmon fillet, skin on or off, cut into 4 portions
- 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
- 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
- 1 lemon, thinly sliced, plus 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

## Instructions

1.  Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
2.  In a small bowl, whisk together 1 tablespoon olive oil, fresh lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped dill, chopped parsley, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
3.  Place salmon portions on one side of the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle half of the herb mixture over the salmon, ensuring each piece is coated.
4.  On the other side of the baking sheet, arrange the trimmed asparagus. Drizzle with the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil and the rest of the herb mixture. Toss gently to coat.
5.  Arrange lemon slices over the salmon fillets and scatter a few slices among the asparagus.
6.  Roast for 12-18 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork, and the asparagus is tender-crisp. Cooking time will vary based on salmon thickness.
7.  Serve immediately.
