## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 bag (10 oz) tortilla chips
- 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup chopped red onion
- 1/2 cup diced jalapeños (optional)
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
- 1/2 cup salsa
- 1/4 cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spatula. Drain off any excess grease.
2. Stir in the taco seasoning and 1/2 cup of water. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
3. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
4. Spread the tortilla chips evenly on a large baking sheet.
5. Spoon the seasoned ground beef mixture over the tortilla chips.
6. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the beef. Add chopped red onion and jalapeños if using.
7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
8. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with fresh cilantro.
9. Serve immediately with salsa and sour cream on the side.
