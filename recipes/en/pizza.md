I love making pizza at home, especially when I'm craving something comforting and customizable. It might seem daunting, but this recipe is surprisingly simple and perfect for any home cook, even if you're just starting out. The aroma of baking pizza filling your kitchen is truly unbeatable, and the taste of fresh, homemade pizza is so much better than delivery.

## Ingredients

- 1 ball of pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce
- 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- Your favorite pizza toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, etc.)
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Pinch of dried oregano (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
2. Lightly flour a clean surface. Stretch or roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness and shape.
3. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel dusted with cornmeal or onto a piece of parchment paper on a baking sheet.
4. Brush the edges of the dough with olive oil.
5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
6. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
7. Arrange your desired toppings over the cheese.
8. If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizza onto the hot stone. If using a baking sheet, place the sheet directly into the oven.
9. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
10. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
