I love pizza nights! There's something so comforting about a warm, cheesy pizza fresh from the oven. This recipe is my go-to for a quick and easy homemade pizza that never disappoints. You don't need to be a professional chef to whip this up; it's surprisingly straightforward and incredibly rewarding. Get ready to impress yourself and your loved ones with this simple, delicious creation!

## Ingredients

- 1 pound pizza dough, store-bought or homemade
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, etc.)
- Pinch of dried oregano (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
2. Lightly flour a clean surface and stretch or roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness and shape.
3. Carefully transfer the dough to a pizza peel dusted with cornmeal or a lightly oiled baking sheet.
4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the Parmesan cheese.
6. Add your desired toppings evenly over the cheese.
7. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with oregano, if using.
8. Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or place the baking sheet in the oven.
9. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
10. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
