## Ingredients

- 2 packages instant ramen noodles (discard seasoning packets)
- 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
- 1/4 cup tahini (or sesame paste)
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon Sriracha (optional, for heat)
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 2 soft-boiled eggs, halved (optional)
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cook ramen noodles according to package directions in a large pot of boiling water until al dente. Drain well and set aside.
2. While noodles cook, prepare the broth. In a medium saucepan, combine chicken or vegetable broth, tahini, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, and Sriracha (if using).
3. Bring the broth mixture to a gentle simmer over medium heat, whisking constantly until well combined and smooth. Do not boil vigorously.
4. Divide the cooked and drained ramen noodles evenly between two serving bowls.
5. Carefully ladle the hot sesame broth over the noodles in each bowl.
6. Garnish each bowl with sliced green onions, halved soft-boiled eggs (if using), and a sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds. Serve immediately.
