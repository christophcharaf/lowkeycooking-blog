## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef (80/20 lean)
- 1 packet (1 oz) taco seasoning mix
- 1/2 cup water
- 8 hard taco shells or small flour tortillas
- 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
- 2 medium tomatoes, diced
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup sour cream (optional)
- 1/4 cup salsa (optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and cook, breaking it apart with a spatula, until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
2. Drain any excess fat from the skillet.
3. Stir in the taco seasoning mix and water. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium-low and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the liquid has mostly absorbed.
4. While the beef simmers, prepare your toppings: shred the lettuce, dice the tomatoes, and have the shredded cheese ready.
5. Warm the taco shells according to package directions (typically in a microwave for 30 seconds or oven at 350°F for 5-7 minutes). If using tortillas, warm them in a dry skillet or microwave.
6. Assemble the tacos: Spoon the seasoned ground beef into the warm taco shells or tortillas. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese. Add sour cream and salsa if desired. Serve immediately.
