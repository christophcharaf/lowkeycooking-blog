## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 1/2 cup water
- 8 oz tortilla chips
- 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
- 1/4 cup chopped red onion
- 1/4 cup sliced jalapeños (optional)
- 1/4 cup sour cream
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a spatula, until browned. Drain off any excess grease.
3. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
4. Spread tortilla chips in a single layer on a baking sheet.
5. Spoon the seasoned ground beef mixture evenly over the tortilla chips.
6. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the beef.
7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
8. Top with chopped tomatoes, red onion, jalapeños (if using), sour cream, and cilantro.
9. Serve immediately.
