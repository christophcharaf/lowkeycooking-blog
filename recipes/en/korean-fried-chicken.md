## Ingredients

- 2 lbs chicken drumsticks and/or thighs, cut into smaller pieces if desired
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup cornstarch
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 large egg
- 1 cup cold water (or club soda for extra crispiness)
- 4 cups vegetable oil, for frying

**For the Sauce:**
- 1/4 cup gochujang (Korean chili paste)
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 3 tbsp brown sugar (or corn syrup/honey)
- 1 tbsp minced garlic
- 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp water

**For Garnish:**
- 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
- 2 green onions, sliced

## Instructions

1.  **Prepare Chicken:** Pat chicken pieces thoroughly dry with paper towels. In a large bowl, combine chicken with 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp grated ginger, 2 minced garlic cloves, 1 tsp salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Toss to coat and let marinate for at least 15 minutes, or up to 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
2.  **Make the Batter:** In another large bowl, whisk together all-purpose flour, cornstarch, and baking powder. In a separate small bowl, whisk the egg with cold water. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. A few lumps are okay. Do not overmix.
3.  **Prepare the Sauce:** In a small saucepan, combine gochujang, 2 tbsp soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, 1 tbsp minced garlic, 1 tsp grated ginger, sesame oil, and 1 tbsp water. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring constantly until the sugar dissolves and the sauce thickens slightly, about 3-5 minutes. Remove from heat.
4.  **First Fry:** Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large heavy-bottomed pot to 325°F (160°C). Dip each marinated chicken piece into the batter, ensuring it's fully coated, letting excess drip off. Carefully lower chicken pieces into the hot oil, frying in batches to avoid overcrowding, for 6-8 minutes until lightly golden and cooked through. Remove chicken to a wire rack set over a baking sheet to drain.
5.  **Second Fry:** Increase oil temperature to 375°F (190°C). Return the chicken to the hot oil for a second fry, again in batches, for another 3-5 minutes until deep golden brown and extra crispy. This double-frying is key for maximum crispiness.
6.  **Sauce and Serve:** Immediately transfer the hot, crispy chicken to a large mixing bowl. Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and toss gently with tongs until each piece is thoroughly coated.
7.  **Garnish:** Transfer the sauced chicken to a serving platter. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Serve hot and enjoy the crunch!
