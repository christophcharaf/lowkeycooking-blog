## Ingredients

- 3 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch black pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh chives (optional, for garnish)

## Instructions

1.  Crack the eggs into a small mixing bowl.
2.  Add a pinch of salt and black pepper to the eggs.
3.  Whisk vigorously with a fork or whisk until the yolks and whites are completely combined and slightly frothy, but not foamy.
4.  Heat an 8-inch non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
5.  Add the butter to the pan and swirl until it melts and coats the bottom, just before it begins to brown.
6.  Pour the whisked eggs into the hot pan.
7.  Immediately begin stirring the eggs with a rubber spatula in a circular motion, pushing cooked egg from the edges towards the center, for about 15-20 seconds.
8.  Stop stirring and let the omelette cook undisturbed for another 15-20 seconds, allowing the bottom to set and the top to remain slightly moist and creamy.
9.  Gently tap the handle of the pan to loosen the omelette, or use the spatula to ensure it's not sticking.
10. With the spatula, fold one-third of the omelette from the far side towards the center.
11. Tilt the pan and roll the omelette onto a plate, forming a neat, elongated cylinder.
12. Garnish with fresh chives, if desired, and serve immediately.
