I love finding recipes that make cleanup a breeze, and this one-pan tilapia is definitely a winner for me. It’s so satisfying to get a healthy, flavorful meal on the table with minimal fuss and just one pan to wash. The bright lemon and fresh herbs really complement the delicate tilapia and crisp spring vegetables, making it feel special enough for guests but easy enough for any weeknight. Even if you're new to cooking, you won't be intimidated by this simple assembly and bake method.

## Ingredients

- 4 (6-ounce) tilapia fillets
- 1 pound asparagus, trimmed
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 lemon, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper for easy cleanup.
2. In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, parsley, dill, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Add the trimmed asparagus, halved cherry tomatoes, and broccoli florets to the bowl with the dressing. Toss gently to coat the vegetables evenly.
4. Arrange the coated vegetables in a single layer on one side of the prepared baking sheet.
5. Place the tilapia fillets on the other side of the baking sheet. Drizzle any remaining dressing over the fish.
6. Top each tilapia fillet with a few thin slices of lemon.
7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the tilapia is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork, and the vegetables are tender-crisp.
8. Serve immediately, with extra lemon wedges if desired.
