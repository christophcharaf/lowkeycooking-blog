Oh, how I love these little bites of sunshine! When I'm craving something fresh, flavorful, and incredibly easy, these Spring Citrus Chicken Empanaditas are my go-to. They're perfect for a quick lunch, a picnic, or even a fun appetizer for friends – and best of all, they come together in a flash!

## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded cooked chicken (from a rotisserie chicken works great!)
- 1/4 cup finely diced red onion
- 1/4 cup finely diced yellow bell pepper
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 package (10-12 discs) pre-made empanada dough discs
- 1 large egg, beaten

## Instructions

1.  Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2.  In a medium skillet over medium heat, warm the olive oil. Add the diced red onion and yellow bell pepper. Sauté for 3-4 minutes until they are softened. Remove the skillet from the heat.
3.  In a medium bowl, combine the shredded chicken, sautéed vegetables, lemon zest, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix everything together well.
4.  Lay out the empanada dough discs on a clean surface. Spoon about 1-2 tablespoons of the chicken mixture onto one half of each disc.
5.  Fold the other half of the dough over the filling to create a semicircle. Use a fork to firmly crimp the edges together, sealing each empanadita. Place them on the prepared baking sheet.
6.  Brush the tops of the empanaditas with the beaten egg. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until they are golden brown and puffed up. Serve warm and enjoy!
