## Ingredients

- 1 cup (approx. 30-45g) your favorite dry cereal
- 1/2 cup (120ml) cold milk (dairy or non-dairy)
- Optional: 1/4 cup fresh berries
- Optional: 1 tsp granulated sugar or honey

## Instructions

1.  Select your preferred dry cereal and a clean cereal bowl.
2.  Carefully pour the desired amount of dry cereal into the bowl. For a standard serving, measure out 1 cup (or use a kitchen scale for precise portioning).
3.  Pour the cold milk over the cereal until it reaches your preferred level, typically just covering the cereal.
4.  If desired, add any optional toppings such as fresh berries, a sprinkle of sugar, or a drizzle of honey.
5.  Grab an eating spoon and enjoy your perfectly simple cereal bowl immediately.
