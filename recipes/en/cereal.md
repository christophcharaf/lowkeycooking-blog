## Ingredients

- 1 cup your favorite cereal
- 1/2 cup cold milk (dairy or non-dairy)
- Optional: fresh fruit, nuts, or a drizzle of honey

## Instructions

1. Select your preferred cereal and a clean bowl.
2. Pour 1 cup of your chosen cereal into the bowl.
3. Carefully pour 1/2 cup of cold milk over the cereal.
4. If desired, add any optional toppings such as sliced fruit, a handful of nuts, or a drizzle of honey.
5. Grab a spoon and enjoy your delicious breakfast immediately.
