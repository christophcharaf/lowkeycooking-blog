Spring is in the air, and so are these incredibly delicious fritters! I absolutely adore how quickly these come together in the air fryer, making a healthy and flavorful meal without any fuss or excess oil. The creamy avocado crema is truly the perfect bright counterpoint to the savory, veggie-packed fritters.

## Ingredients

**For the Fritters:**
- 1 cup fresh or frozen (thawed) corn kernels
- 1 medium zucchini, grated and squeezed of excess water
- 1 medium carrot, grated
- 2 scallions, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup fine cornmeal
- 1 large egg
- 1/4 cup milk (any kind)
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- Olive oil spray for air fryer

**For the Avocado Crema:**
- 1 ripe avocado
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
- 2 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt or sour cream
- 1-2 tablespoons water (for thinning)
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1.  **Prepare Vegetables:** Grate the zucchini and carrot. Place the grated zucchini in a clean kitchen towel or paper towels and squeeze out as much excess water as possible – this is key for crispy fritters! Thinly slice the scallions. If using frozen corn, ensure it's thawed.
2.  **Mix Fritter Batter:** In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cornmeal, baking powder, salt, and black pepper. In a separate small bowl, lightly whisk the egg and milk. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Gently fold in the corn, squeezed zucchini, carrot, and scallions.
3.  **Make Avocado Crema:** In a small food processor or using a fork and bowl, combine the ripe avocado, lime juice, chopped cilantro, Greek yogurt (or sour cream), and a pinch of salt. Blend or mash until smooth. Add water one tablespoon at a time until you reach your desired creamy consistency. Set aside.
4.  **Preheat Air Fryer:** Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C) for about 3-5 minutes. Lightly spray the air fryer basket with olive oil spray.
5.  **Cook Fritters:** Drop heaping spoonfuls (about 2 tablespoons each) of the fritter batter into the preheated air fryer basket, ensuring not to overcrowd it. Leave a little space between each fritter. Lightly spray the tops of the fritters with olive oil.
6.  **Air Fry:** Cook for 8-10 minutes, flipping the fritters halfway through, until they are golden brown and cooked through. The exact time may vary depending on your air fryer model and fritter thickness.
7.  **Serve:** Transfer cooked fritters to a plate and repeat with the remaining batter. Serve the warm fritters immediately with a dollop of the fresh avocado crema. Enjoy!
