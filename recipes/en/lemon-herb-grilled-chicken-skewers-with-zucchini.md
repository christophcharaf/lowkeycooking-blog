There's nothing quite like the ease and flavor of grilled skewers, especially when the weather is warm! This recipe for Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken Skewers with Zucchini is a personal favorite because it's incredibly quick to throw together, healthy, and bursting with fresh, bright flavors that satisfy everyone at the table.

## Ingredients

- 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 2 medium zucchini, cut into 1/2-inch thick half-moons
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano (or Italian seasoning)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- Wooden or metal skewers (if using wooden, soak in water for 30 minutes)

## Instructions

1.  In a large mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper. Whisk well to create the marinade.
2.  Add the chicken cubes and zucchini pieces to the bowl with the marinade. Toss gently to ensure everything is evenly coated. Let marinate for at least 10 minutes (or up to 30 minutes in the refrigerator for deeper flavor).
3.  Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using a grill pan, lightly oil it.
4.  Thread the marinated chicken and zucchini alternately onto the skewers. Don't pack them too tightly, allowing for even cooking.
5.  Place the skewers on the preheated grill. Cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through (internal temperature of 165°F or 74°C) and the zucchini is tender-crisp with nice grill marks.
6.  Remove from the grill and let rest for a couple of minutes before serving. Enjoy these vibrant skewers as a standalone meal or with a side of rice or a fresh salad!
