## Ingredients

- 1 pound store-bought potato gnocchi
- 8 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1/4 cup fresh sage leaves
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
- Salt, to taste
- Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Add the gnocchi to the boiling water and cook according to package directions, usually 2-3 minutes, until they float to the surface.
3. While the gnocchi cooks, melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Add the sage leaves to the skillet and cook until they become crisp, about 2-3 minutes. Be careful not to burn the butter.
5. Continue cooking the butter until it turns a nutty brown color and smells fragrant, about 5-7 minutes.
6. Using a slotted spoon, transfer the cooked gnocchi directly from the boiling water into the skillet with the brown butter and sage.
7. Add the grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet.
8. Gently toss the gnocchi with the brown butter, sage, and Parmesan until well coated.
9. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
10. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese.
